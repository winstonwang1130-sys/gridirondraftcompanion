About Us

We are a group of passionate American football fans and data-driven developers who built GridironDraftCompanion.com with one simple mission: eliminate the anxiety of fantasy football draft day.

Every year, millions of fantasy players scramble to build cheat sheets from outdated rankings, conflicting expert opinions, and spreadsheets that don't account for their specific league settings. We've been there — staring at a generic Top 200 list that doesn't reflect our 14-team Half-PPR league, wondering if we're about to make a pick we'll regret all season.

That frustration is why we created this site. Our free Cheat Sheet Generator uses automated ranking algorithms that adjust for your exact league size and scoring format, giving you a personalized draft board in seconds. No signup required, no paywall, no nonsense.

What We Believe

We believe fantasy football tools should be accessible to everyone — from the commissioner running their first league to the veteran chasing their fifth championship belt. Great draft preparation shouldn't cost money or require a computer science degree.

We also believe in transparency. Our rankings are based on publicly available ADP data and statistical models, not gut feelings or sponsorship deals.

What We Offer

  • Free Cheat Sheet Generator: Customized draft rankings for 8, 10, 12, and 14-team leagues in Standard, PPR, and Half-PPR formats.
  • PDF Export: Print or save your cheat sheet for draft day.
  • Regular Updates: Rankings refreshed daily during peak draft season via our automated data pipeline, reflecting real-time ADP shifts from active fantasy platforms.

Our Team

GridironDraftCompanion is maintained by a small team of developers, data analysts, and fantasy football addicts who spend way too much time watching film and not enough time sleeping during the NFL season. We're not affiliated with the NFL, any fantasy platform, or any player agency. We're just fans who love the game and love helping others win.
