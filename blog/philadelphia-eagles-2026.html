Philadelphia Eagles Fantasy Football Guide 2026: Barkley and Brown Dominate

Published May 25, 2026 · Philadelphia Eagles · 7 min read

Saquon Barkley's first full season in Philadelphia was everything Eagles fans dreamed of — 1,800+ scrimmage yards and 15 touchdowns. Combined with A.J. Brown and Jalen Hurts, Philly remains a fantasy powerhouse.

Saquon Barkley — RB · ADP ~4 · Draft Range: Round 1

Barkley is a top-5 pick in all formats. Behind the Eagles' elite offensive line, he should repeat as an RB1 with 1,500+ yards and double-digit TDs. Don't overthink the 1.04 pick.

A.J. Brown — WR · ADP ~19 · Draft Range: Round 2

Brown is a steady WR1 with 1,000+ yard potential every season. His red-zone role and target share make him a safe Round 2 pick.

Jalen Hurts — QB · ADP ~41 · Draft Range: Round 4–5

Hurts' rushing TD production (10+ per year) keeps him in the QB1 conversation. Target him in Round 4 if you want a dual-threat QB without paying Allen/Jackson prices.

Devonta Smith — WR · ADP ~55 · Draft Range: Round 5–7

Smith is the WR2 in Philly but still a quality flex option. 80+ catches and 900+ yards are within reach. Value pick in PPR leagues.
