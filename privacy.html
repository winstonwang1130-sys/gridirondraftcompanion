Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 31, 2026

Gridiron Draft Companion ("we," "us," or "our") operates the website gridiron-draft-companion.online. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you visit our website.

Information We Process

Browser-Based Tool Data: Our Cheat Sheet Generator runs entirely in your browser. Your custom league settings and generated ranking boards are processed locally and are never transmitted to our servers or stored in any database.

Voluntary Information: When you use our Contact form, we collect the information you voluntarily provide (such as your name, email address, and message content) solely to respond to your inquiries.

Automatically Collected Information & Cookies

Like most websites, we automatically collect certain non-personal identification information to optimize site performance, including browser type, operating system, referring URL, and aggregated geography (country level). This is handled via standard server logs and analytics tools.

Google AdSense and Third-Party Advertising

We use Google AdSense to serve advertisements when you visit our website. Google and its third-party vendors or ad networks use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this website or other websites on the Internet.

  • Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to you based on your visit to our site and/or other sites on the Internet.
  • Third-party vendors or ad networks may also place and read cookies on your browser, or use web beacons to collect information as a result of ad serving on our site.
  • You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings. Alternatively, you can opt out of a third-party vendor's use of cookies for personalized advertising by visiting www.aboutads.info.

GDPR Compliance (EEA, UK, and Switzerland)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), United Kingdom, or Switzerland, you have certain data protection rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). Our legal basis for processing data is legitimate interest (site optimization) and explicit consent (when you interact with our consent banners or submit a contact form).

You have the right to access, rectify, or erase your personal data (such as contact emails). To exercise these rights, contact us at gridirondraftcompanion@outlook.com.

US State Privacy Compliance (CCPA, CPRA, and GPP)

If you are a resident of California or other US states with active consumer privacy laws, you are granted specific rights regarding your personal information under the CPRA and the Global Privacy Platform (GPP) framework:

  • Right to Know & Access: You can request disclosure of what personal information we collect.
  • Right to Opt-Out: We do not sell your personal data. However, the use of third-party advertising cookies may be classified as "sharing" for cross-context behavioral advertising. You can exercise your right to opt-out via our site's Cookie Consent banner or your browser's Global Privacy Control (GPC) signal.
  • Right to Delete & Correct: You can request the deletion or correction of your email correspondence data by contacting gridirondraftcompanion@outlook.com with the subject line "US Privacy Request."

Children's Privacy & Security

Our website is not directed at children under the age of 13. We implement industry-standard operational measures to secure the limited data we handle. However, no internet transmission is 100% secure.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.

Contact Us

If you have any questions, please contact us at gridirondraftcompanion@outlook.com or visit our Contact page.
